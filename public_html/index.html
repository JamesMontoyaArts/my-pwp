Rules

  1. TELL YOUR FRIENDS!. The more people we have the better!
  2. The majority of the work MUST be created LIVE! This allows the audience to see the creations come alive from start to finish.
  3. Artists bring their own supplies and drop cloth. Any style or medium is acceptable.
  4. Artists have three hours to create while the public vote throughout the night by buying and enjoying craft beer!
  5. Winner of vote count takes all artist entry fees!

Live Auction

At the end of the three hours of creating the participating artists will put up their pieces for live auction that night in the tap room! The artist sets the starting bid, the potential buyers bid by raising their hand,we increase the bid incrementally as bids are cast and the highest bidder at the end wins the piece! The artist also donates a portion of the sale to that night's community partner or cause!
